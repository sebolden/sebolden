About

My research looks at the dynamics between users and administrators on social media platforms, especially in the context of content regulation policies. Using a critical theoretical lens and mixed qualitative methods, I seek to answer questions about the evolution of social media platforms’ policies over time; the sociopolitical conditions that surround and potentially influence platforms’ policies; the strategies that platform administrators (and/or moderators) use to enforce policies; the accessibility and/or transparency of platforms’ policies; and user responses to policy decisions and/or enforcement.

In addition to this research, I'm interested in practices of data visualization and data feminism. I leverage creative design and computational methods to develop and share blogs, infographics, and other projects that foreground topics pertaining to data justice.
