About

My research investigates policymaking, governance, and content regulation on social media sites such as Reddit. My work considers the social and political conditions that underlie policy decisions, regulation strategies, and changes that occur in these arenas. Ultimately, the goal of my research is to assess the efficacy of platform policies in the regulation of harmful content.

In addition to this research, I'm interested in practices of data visualization and data feminism. I leverage creative design and computational methods to develop and share blogs, infographics, and other projects that foreground topics pertaining to data justice.
